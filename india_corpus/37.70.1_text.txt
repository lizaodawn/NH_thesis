the 'zathenes,' according to democritus, is an amber-coloured stone found in media, and if it is ground with palm wine and saffron softens like wax and has a most agreeable smell. the 'zamilampis,' zamilar which is found in the euphrates, is like the marble from the island of marmara, but is greyish-green in the centre. 'zoraniscaea' is said to be a gem found in the river indus and used by the magi, but, apart from this, nothing is reported about it.
